Aboriginal innovation meets STEM

Cultural foundation
Aboriginal innovation focus
The Brewarrina fish traps (Baiame's Ngunnhu) are among the oldest human-made structures on Earth. The game teaches students that Aboriginal knowledge has always been world-class engineering.
